Asset Management

Asset Management

As information technology organizations grow and mature, there is a greater need to streamline asset management to deliver business value. Our solutions are built to improve your operational efficiency by addressing the core challenges you face as an asset management company.

Dependence on external data

Obtaining and seamlessly processing data from multiple sources i.e., custodian (position), market info (price, corporate actions), RTA (subscriptions & redemptions) and credit rating agencies (ratings, benchmarks).

Compliance

The dynamic nature of compliance - based on domicile and preponderance of rules.

Timeliness and accuracy of NAVs

The need to generate hundreds of NAVs & take care of NAV based fee complexities within a stipulated time while being accurate to the nth decimal.

Liquidity management

The necessity to invest subscriptions on time, calculate cash demand for funds to service redemptions and the need to predict investible funds accurately.

Securities Processing Firms

Securities processing firms face challenges with transaction volumes, cross-border trades, complex financial products and quick settlement Turnaround Times. They require solutions that cover an entire gamut of functions inclusive of trade execution through confirmation, settlement and accounting. Our solution ensures the benefits of operational efficiency & flexibility, and enables a variety of functions.

Handling of multiple clients across multiple geographies (time zones)

The ability to configure the shop floor either on a client-centric or a process-centric manner

The use of reconciliation and auto-checking tools to ensure that all NAVs and other critical figures are reconciled and validated before transmittal to clients.

Adhering to SLAs ( monitoring, escalation and management)

The ability to service multiple clients through one set of reference data (multi-tenanting)

Trustees

Trustees

Trustee companies are facing increased risks due to the lack of access to real-time scheme data that helps them make crucial decisions. Risk is key as these companies face a growing list of responsibilities & need to adapt to changing market circumstances while balancing the pressures from both the sponsor and the regulator. Trustees want to ensure governance structures focus on what’s important and embrace a risk-based approach to funding, where all investments, contributions and covenant avenues are explored to deliver better member outcomes.

Our trustee solution, mPower has been carefully developed to ensure process efficiencies and quick turnaround time for crucial data. The solution enables you to:

Capture and process all client transactions.

Shadow processes and pro-actively compare results against those provided by clients.

Manage multiple clients and multiple businesses (unit trusts, private debt, wills and estates)
