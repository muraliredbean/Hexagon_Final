Hexagon mPower is an end-to-end investment management product designed to double operational efficiency and provide an unmatched user experience.

Modular by Design

The Front Office SuiteFoS

Hexagon mPower Front Office supports decision making, trade origination and trade flow confirmation through a set of blotters. It also provides for personalization of these blotters. Pre-trade compliance is weaved throughout the trade flow at appropriate trade points. A scenario analyzer to verifies the impact of trades on compliance and exposure. Portfolio metrics is a crucial part of the Product.

The Back Office SuiteBoS

Hexagon mPower Back Office receives trades from various origination points and houses them in a handover blotter. Based on deployment settings, accounting is accomplished automatically or upon user choice. The product performs any 'allowed' trade adjustments and also fulfills any period end audit observations. Valuations and income recognition are completely automated based on market practice, chosen policies and IFRS, regional variants of the same are fully supported. Apart from operational reports, statutory reporting for selected countries are pre-packaged. Post trade compliance is fully automated with the ability to inform appropriate responsibility points via alerts or email.

End to EndE2E

Hexagon mPower Enterprise is an end to end investment management product, comprehensively covering front-to-back office requirements. Front office, Mid office, Back office activities are functionally segregated to obtain efficiencies and also to provide adequate checks and balances.
