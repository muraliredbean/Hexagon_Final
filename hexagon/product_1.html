The Hexagon Mobility platform is aimed at facilitating better functioning within the Mutual Fund industry. This solution benefits all industry stakeholders by enhancing the underlying business relationships between the manufacturer, the distributor and the investor. Based on disintermediation, the product demystifies the mutual fund industry to the average person and makes investment a safe and desirable option for anyone with a smart phone.

Ezee Advice enables mutual fund product distributors and ﬁnancial advisors to grow a large retail distribution network without extensive physical travel. The desktop dashboard enables the advisor to manage large sums, make informed transfer decisions, recommend transactions to investors in his network and bulk upload his existing investors onto the Ezee Advice platform. The app enables direct communication through its messaging system. Advisors could be individuals or large organizations, therefore creating a more level playing ﬁeld.

Ezee Invest allows investors to invest money in a fund of their choosing, with or without the help of an advisor. Investors can search for advisors by area and choose to act upon suggested transactions made by their advisor. Similar to a social network, investors can add advisors to their network, view associates of their advisor as well as rate an advisor. Investors can choose how much information to share with their advisors and transactions can be completed through internet banking, debit card, debit mandate, fund transfer, NEFT, or RTGS.
