End to EndE2E

Hexagon mPower Enterprise is our end to end flagship investment management product that comprehensively covers front-to-back office requirements. Designed to double operational efficiency and provide an unmatched user experience, Front office, Mid office, Back office activities are functionally segregated to provide adequate checks and balances.

Front OfficeFoS

Hexagon mPower Front Office supports decision making, trade origination & trade flow confirmation. Pre-trade compliance is weaved throughout the trade flow at appropriate trade points. A scenario analyzer verifies the impact of trades on compliance & exposure. Portfolio metrics are also a crucial part of this product.

Back OfficeBoS

Hexagon mPower Back Office receives trades from various origination points and houses them. The product performs 'allowed' trade adjustments and also fulfills any period end audit observations. Accounting, valuations & income as well as post trade compliance are automated based on various factors.

This audit friendly recon robot automates the entire lifecycle of complex, enterprise wide reconciliation requirements involving structured & unstructured data.
